Add an EFP (Ethereum Follow Protocol) indexer plugin. Enable it by including `efp` in the `PLUGINS` environment variable (mainnet ENS namespace) to index EFP list NFTs, records, tags, and account metadata — plus the `eth.efp.list` ENS text record — into ENSDb's `efp_*` tables.
